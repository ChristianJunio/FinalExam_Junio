In FASHION, nothing is perfect

and everything is perfect

Blogging is something that I’ve been dreaming to pursue since I can remember. I’ve been trying to start a blog for years but there are a lot of factors that is holding me back. There are a lot of ‘What If’s’ and ‘Doubts’ that keeps on distracting me to start this. But now my dreams to start this dream of mine is now igniting. So instead of doubting myself and being negative I started to make a step to reach my goals.

I have decided to create a blog that is very close to my heart and that is a FASHION blog. FASHION is what makes me who I am and what makes me happy and inspired. FASHION is what makes my soul complete and satisfied.

The goal of this blog is to share what I’m currently thinking about in terms of trends, a garment or accessory, street fashion, or something that I’ve seen on television or a magazine. Also, I would like to share a few fashion tips and creative projects that I’m working on, have done, or planning to do [fashion journal, etc.] and even a review of the latest fashion book that I’m currently reading or even a current issue of a high fashion magazine. And along the way, I want to share a few fashion stories of my own, whether it be good or bad to share my ideas and talent while I am inspiring others.

Emotion in Fashion

“For me in fashion there are main emotions that I expressed through it.” - Christian Junio

ANGER – where I can show that I’m a fire in blaze through the expression of my face and my outfit.

SAD – where I can convey to the people around me my emotions without saying something, but through seeing my outfit.

FIERCE – where I can be a magician not by showing some tricks but giving them an aesthetically pleasing outfit.

What are the things you need to consider in fashion?

The combination of colors – it is very important to consider the combination of colors when choosing your clothes because in that way you will catch the attention of the people.

The Photography - you want a better picture of yours? Then be a pro in shooting of your own picture because the people will love your photos when it is in the right angle.

The place – ​you should consider the place where you will take your pictures because it is better if if the ambiance is fit to your outfit.

The Photography - the outfit you’re wearing should be detailed because it can ruin your day. For example: A tiny people should wear vertical stripes so that it will become taller from their original height. A fat people should not wear horizontal stripes because their body will become larger.
